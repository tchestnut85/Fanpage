Coheed and Cambria fansite!

Coheed and Cambria have been one of my favorite bands for years. They are a progressive/alternative rock band. My wife and I have listened to them together ever since we first started dating, and we have been fans ever since. We have seen them in concert more times than I can count over the years. Coheed and Cambria have been around since the early 2000s, and have released 9 albums. Most of the albums and songs are about a sci-fi story, and the main characters are what the band is named after.

Check them out on their official website!

Band Members

  • Claudio Sanchez - vocals and guitar
  • Travis Stever - guitar
  • Josh Eppard - drums
  • Zach Cooper - bass
